Ho ho ho!

It's that time of year again where many people count down the days to Christmas.

In the spirit of an open and friendly environment for beginners and experts alike to apply and hone their skills, this holiday season we at OverTheWire once again treat you to

The OverTheWire Advent Bonanza CTF!

Every day of the advent season, you will be greeted with a new treat in the form of a challenge. You can compete as a team and there will be secrets and surprises littered throughout. Sign up your team, join us on Discord (or IRC) and follow the updates on twitter @OverTheWireCTF.

About:
OverTheWire (OTW) is the oldest hacker wargame community on the internet. It boasts several pwn2own winners amongst its alumni. The goal of OTW is to provide an environment to learn and practice security and coding principles through a hands on approach. Read Phrack Issue 0x45 for a historical perspective on how we came to be. You are free to drop in any time at http://overthewire.org as well.
